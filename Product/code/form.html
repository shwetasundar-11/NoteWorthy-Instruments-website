NoteWorthy Instrument Application

Hello! Thank you so much for reaching out to NoteWorthy! Please fill out the form below so we can try to connect you with an instrument(s). We have a limited number of violins, violas, and clarinets.


If you are able, there is a $20 deposit on each of our instruments. You can use the instrument(s) for as long as you'd like, and when you no longer need it, you can return it and get your deposit back. This is to ensure that Noteworthy is sustainable and can assist many students with our limited instrument supply over the years.


Thank you for using NoteWorthy! We appreciate your time.
